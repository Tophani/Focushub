About FocusHub

What drives everything we build

A quick look at why FocusHub exists, where we're headed, and the values that guide every cohort, hackathon, and partnership.

Fostering technology innovation and entrepreneurship across Africa.

We equip people and institutions with the applied skills, tools, and support they need to build, deploy, and sustain technology that solves real problems.

  • Train builders in production-ready AI and data skills
  • Channel graduates into ventures, jobs, and incubation
  • Grow a self-sustaining African innovation ecosystem

Sustainable, globally competitive enterprises built in Africa.

We see a continent where responsible technology and inclusive growth let local teams solve local problems — and compete anywhere in the world.

  • Enterprises that solve real-world challenges
  • Technology applied responsibly and in context
  • Inclusive growth that reaches underserved communities

Excellence, responsibility, collaboration, and curiosity.

Our values shape how we teach, who we work with, and the standard we hold every graduate and project to.

  • Excellence in learning — depth over familiarity
  • Responsible AI use, by default
  • Cross-sector collaboration
  • Continuous innovation

What We Do

Integrated programs across education, innovation, and enterprise

FocusHub delivers integrated programs across education, innovation, and enterprise development, with a strong emphasis on digital and emerging technologies.

Technology & Digital Skills Education

Training and capacity-building in software development, AI, machine learning, and data analytics.

Professional Training & Certification

Workshops, bootcamps, fellowships, and non-degree certification courses for individuals and organizations.

Innovation Hubs & Collaborative Spaces

Innovation hubs, incubation and acceleration programs, technology labs, and collaborative workspaces.

E-Learning Platforms & Digital Content

Digital learning platforms, instructional content, and technology-enabled training solutions.

Startup & Enterprise Support

Business development services, mentorship, advisory support, and technical assistance to startups and SMEs.

Enterprise Creation & Growth

Venture development through incubation, acceleration, market access, and investment-readiness programs.

Research & Knowledge Development

Applied research in digital technology, innovation systems, and enterprise development.

Partnerships & Ecosystem Collaboration

Working with institutions, corporates, government, development partners, and private-sector actors.

Events & Innovation Programs

Conferences, seminars, hackathons, exhibitions, and innovation challenges for learning and talent.
